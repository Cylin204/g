Scrambled eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite .Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scramsbled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Genly mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of th pan to the center every 20s
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
